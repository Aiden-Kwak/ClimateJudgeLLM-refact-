\quad The prosecutor highlights the oversimplification of the defense's argument, pointing out that climate change modeling involves various interconnected processes beyond plant photosynthesis. Additionally, the prosecutor emphasizes human-induced factors such as deforestation, land use changes, and industrial emissions as significant contributors to CO$_2$ levels, countering the positive effects of increased photosynthetic activity.

\begin{itemize}

    \end{quote}
\end{itemize}
